💡 Quick Tips

  • Use an before vowel sounds — "an apple"
  • Avoid double negatives — "I don't know anything"
  • Use they're (they are), there (place), their (possessive)
  • Every sentence starts with a capital letter
  • Subject-verb: "He is" not "He are"
  • Use you're = you are, your = possessive

🔬 How It Works

  1. Your text is sent to the Flask backend
  2. pyspellchecker flags spelling errors
  3. Rule-based engine checks grammar patterns
  4. Scikit-learn (TF-IDF + Logistic Regression) gives an ML verdict
  5. A score from 0–100 is computed and returned
